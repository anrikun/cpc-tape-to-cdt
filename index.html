Amstrad CPC Tape to CDT Online Converter

Capture and convert standard Amstrad CPC 464 tape block-files into CDT files, supporting regular formats saved from real machines.

Capture from a WAV file
Drag and drop a WAV file here, or click Browse to select one. Then click Play (▶) to start capturing audio from the file.

Capture from audio input
Connect your tape player or audio device to your computer. Then click Capture to start recording audio input from your cassette.
Please note: capturing directly from an audio source may be less stable than capturing from a pre-recorded WAV file. If you encounter a read error, rewind your cassette to the beginning of the block and retry, ensuring the recording volume is set to a reasonable level.
